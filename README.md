## User Roles

### Administrator
- Create, update, and deactivate system users
- Register and manage suppliers
- Record product purchases
- Monitor inventory levels and stock alerts
- Access sales and inventory reports
- Review inventory movements and purchase history

### Employee
- Register product sales
- Consult product availability and stock levels
- Confirm completed sales transactions

---

## Functional Capabilities
- Secure authentication and role-based access control
- Automatic inventory updates for sales and purchases
- Supplier and purchase management
- Sales transaction processing
- Inventory monitoring with low-stock alerts
